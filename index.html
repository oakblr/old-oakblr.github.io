What is a Codefest?

A codefest is a platform that provides the opportunity for aspiring developers to showcase their unique talents to both each other and veterans of the industry. Our codefest is Bangalore's only overnight school hackathon and we plan on making it as fulfilling and rewarding an experience as possible.

group

Themes

With a multitude of impactful themes, the participants will be tackling current technical problems with innovative improvements and solutions.

settings

Rewards

Placement in the top three will guarantee a large monetary reward, and additionally, sponsor themes have their own rewards, which include a large assortment of products and services of equal value.

General themes

Teams are expected to use at least one primary theme for their project but are recommended to choose two or more themes. For more infomation regarding themes please email us at oakcodefest@oakridge.in
  • directions_transitTransportation

    Humans, around the world, commute millions of miles every single day. A significant fraction of our day is spent in traveling. Technology has played a very significant role in enabling more people to commute. Be it in the form discovery, aggregation or purchase, technology has redefined the way we commute. Imagine how technology can human experience even better in day to day transportation and commute. What can technology do make public and private transportation much simpler?

    Examples : Traffic Reduction Software and Autonomous Transport Solutions

  • battery_alertEnergy informatics

    Energy informatics is the field covering the use of information and communication technology to address energy challenges.

    The efficiency of our nation's energy grids, has the ability to affect the course of our lives, and the challenges we face. After all, electricity plays a huge part in everyone's world. The ability to improve our energy informatics is one that is imperative for the future of our entire work. What can technology do to make our energy informatics more efficient?

    Examples : Smart City Solutions, Internet of Things, Micro Grids, and Battery Management Systems

  • accessible Human Assistance

    Computer Science was made to make life easier for humans. In today's world, we have reached the peak of what we are able to achieve using computation. Everyday tasks are becoming easier and easier. With that being said, what more can we achieve? How can we use our computer science skills to make everyday life easier for everyone?

    Examples : Disability Assistant Solutions, Routine Planning Algorithm, and Smart Assistance

  • backupEnhance Solutions

    What separates the human race from other races, is their desire and potential to innovate and simplify the world around them. Our lives are full of complex problems, and through the use of programming, we can solve them. In the same vein, are the solutions we make perfect? How can we use programming to make better and more innovative solutions?

    Examples : Optimizing Pre-made Algorithms, Designing New User Interfaces, etc.

  • videogame_assetGame Development(6-8th Grade)

    Video games are a great way to have fun and learn code at the same time!

    Use your knowledge of coding to make fun and interactive game for everyone to enjoy!

    For this theme, we expect participants to be creative and innovative and have some kind of unique idea or message in it. Participants need to use programming languages and avoid using drag and drop software such as Scratch or other WYSIWYG software.

Prizes Worth ₹2.2 Lakhs up for grabs.

More prizes will be announced soon.

FAQ

  • placeWill I be provided food?
    Yes, meals, snacks and beverages will be provided at the event at no extra cost.
  • filter_dramaDo I have to arrange for an internet connection?
    Internet will be provided at the event, but we recommend you carry a hotspot or dongle for faster and consistent internet.
  • placeDo I have to stay the night?
    We recommend that you stay during the night as it gives you more time to work on your project. However, it is not required to stay the night. If you choose to stay, there will be food, beverages, and bean bags provided.
  • placeWhat languages/software can I use?
    Any language, technology stack, or library can be used to create your solutions, except MIT App Inventor and the like.
  • filter_dramaDo we need to have the idea/solution fully working?
    The entire idea need not be implemented, however, the submission should be functional so that it can be reviewed by the judges.
  • whatshotWho will own the IP (Intellectual Property) rights to the solution?
    The developer/developers of the web/mobile application will have all rights and own the IP of the product. However, all code needs to be in the public domain (open source) so that it can be evaluated by the judges.
  • filter_dramaWho can participate in this event?
    Anyone who is in school and is interested in technology can participate.
  • whatshotDo I have to bring my laptop?
    Yes, you are expected to bring you laptop and charger along with any software or dependencies you will be needing.
  • filter_dramaDo you need to pay for this event?
    Yes, there is a ₹300 registration fee per person. This fee will be donated to charity.
